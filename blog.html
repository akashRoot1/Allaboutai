🤖 About AllAboutAI

AllAboutAI (thatai.me) is an independent blog dedicated to helping professionals harness the power of AI automation. Founded by Akash, a software engineer and automation enthusiast, the site was born out of a simple frustration: there were no good, free, hands-on guides for setting up n8n, Make.com, and Zapier for real-world tasks like job searching.

Every article on this site is written from personal experience — the workflows are ones we actually use, the tools are ones we've actually tested, and the prompts are ones that have genuinely delivered results. No fluff, no affiliate-driven rankings, just practical content that saves you time.

Our focus areas include workflow automation (n8n, Make.com, Zapier), AI productivity tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), developer guides (LangChain, LlamaIndex, vector databases), and job search automation — because landing your next role should be as efficient as everything else you automate.
